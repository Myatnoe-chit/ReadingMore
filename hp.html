Harry Potter Series

Overview

The Harry Potter series is a collection of seven fantasy novels that chronicles the adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. The series revolves around Harry's struggle against the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who aims to conquer the wizarding world and subjugate non-magical people.

About the Author

J.K. Rowling is a British author, best known for writing the Harry Potter series. The books have gained immense popularity, critical acclaim, and commercial success worldwide. Rowling's work has been credited with inspiring a new generation of readers.

Key Themes

  • Good vs. Evil
  • Friendship and Loyalty
  • Bravery and Sacrifice
  • The Power of Love
  • Identity and Belonging
